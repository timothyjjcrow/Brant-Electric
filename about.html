About Brant Electric

Your Trusted Electrical Partner in Humboldt County

Our Story: Over 30 Years of Service

Peter Brant founded Brant Electric in 1982 with a vision to provide reliable and high-quality electrical services to the Humboldt County community. Today, Brant Electric is a proud family-run business, continuing Peter's legacy with a small yet highly experienced and dedicated staff. We are deeply rooted in Arcata and serve the greater Trinidad to Eureka areas.

Our Mission & Values

At Brant Electric, we pride ourselves on our unwavering commitment to Integrity and Quality. We believe in providing honest, transparent services and delivering workmanship that stands the test of time. We are fully licensed (CA License # 406330) and bonded, ensuring our clients have complete peace of mind.

We are also invested in continually keeping up to date on new and changing technology in the electrical field. This allows us to offer modern, efficient, and safe solutions for all your electrical needs, whether residential, commercial, or industrial.

Why We Stand Out

  • Local Expertise: Deep understanding of local needs and regulations in Arcata and Humboldt County.
  • Experienced Team: A small, dedicated team with significant hands-on experience.
  • Customer-Focused: We prioritize clear communication and tailor our services to meet your specific requirements.
  • Commitment to Safety: Adherence to the highest safety standards in all our projects.
