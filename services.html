Our Electrical Services

Comprehensive solutions for all your electrical needs.

Brant Electric offers a wide range of quality services for residential, commercial, and industrial clients throughout Humboldt County. We are invested in continually keeping up to date on new and changing technology to provide you with the best solutions.

Residential, Commercial & Industrial

Complete electrical work tailored for homes, businesses, and industrial facilities. We handle projects of all sizes with precision and care.

Solar/Generators

Harness the power of the sun or ensure backup power. We offer solar panel installations, generator hook-ups, and expert consultations to optimize your system.

New Construction

Electrical planning, estimates, proposals, and installation for new building projects. We work with you from the ground up.

Remodels

Upgrading your space? We provide electrical solutions for remodels, including estimates and proposals to integrate new systems seamlessly.

Telecommunications & Data Wiring

Stay connected. We install and repair phone lines, jacks, and new lines for your computer and data systems.

Troubleshooting

Switches or outlets not working? Circuits out? Breaker tripped? Don't know what to do? Call us for expert troubleshooting and fast solutions.

Repairs/Rewires
